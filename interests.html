I took a picture of my meals everyday for the entire school year and sent it to my family. I won that bet. My love for cooking, baking and food videos has only grown. I've been experimenting a lot with different breads and have tried a lot of recipes in quarantine. I even did the sourdough thing. Here are some of my favorite recipes.
